About

I'm a product designer with three years of experience turning messy, ambiguous problems into products people actually want to use.

My process starts with real research — talking to people, observing behaviour, finding the gap between what people say they do and what they actually do. From there I move fast: sketches, prototypes, tests, iteration.

I've worked across fintech, health, and B2B SaaS — and I've learned that the domain matters less than the mindset. Good design is good thinking, regardless of the industry.

When I'm not designing I'm probably reading dense systems theory, going for long runs, or arguing about typography with strangers online.
