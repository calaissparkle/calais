About

I'm a Product Designer with 6+ years of experience turning messy, ambiguous problems into experiences people actually want to use. Designing products that are clear, considered, and built around real people.

My process starts with real research and data: talking to people, observing behaviour, finding the gap between what people say they do and what they actually do. From there I move fast: sketches, prototypes, tests and iterations.

When I'm not designing I'm probably reading, trying to perfect my coffee and matcha brews, galavanting around Zelda side quests, or at the local community market or gig.
